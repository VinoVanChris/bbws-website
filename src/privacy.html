Privacy Policy

Effective May 19, 2026

Broadway Beer Wine & Spirits ("we", "us", "our") operates the website at broadwaybeerwine.ca. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA, BC) and the federal Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

What We Collect

Information you give us

When you submit the contact form on our website, we collect your name, email address, and the contents of your message. We use this only to respond to your inquiry. Messages are processed through Formspree (formspree.io) and stored on their servers; their privacy policy is available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, we use Google's advertising and analytics tags. When you visit our site, Google may collect your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and other browsing data through cookies. This helps us understand how the site is used and measure the effectiveness of any advertising we run.

We use Google Ads conversion tracking (tag ID: AW-970988484). No personally identifiable information is sent to Google by us; however, Google's own data practices apply. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to questions and enquiries submitted via the contact form
  • To understand how visitors use our website and improve the experience
  • To measure the performance of any advertising we run

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies placed by Google for advertising and analytics purposes. You can opt out of Google's use of cookies by visiting adssettings.google.com, or manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to use this website.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained by Formspree for as long as our account is active. We do not maintain a separate database of contact enquiries beyond our email correspondence. Google Analytics and Ads data is retained per Google's standard retention settings (up to 26 months).

Your Rights

Under PIPA and PIPEDA, you have the right to request access to personal information we hold about you, to correct inaccurate information, and to withdraw consent for its use. To make a request, contact us using the details below. We will respond within 30 days.

Children

This website is intended for adults aged 19 and over. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 19.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
