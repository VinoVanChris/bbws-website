Our Story

What Sets
Us Apart.

We have been part of the Kitsilano community since 1986. The shop has evolved over the decades, but the mission has not. We carry a curated selection of wine, beer, and spirits without the pretension.


Whether you are after a rare single malt or a reliable local craft tallboy, we have the right bottle waiting for you.

Curated Selection

Each week we taste dozens of beers, wines, and spirits to build a selection that is uniquely ours — hard to find bottles, cult classics, and your everyday Netflix sips.

Corporate Gifting

Office delivery, staff appreciation gifts, and custom holiday packages. We also provide cellar consulting for the executive building a curated wine or scotch collection.
