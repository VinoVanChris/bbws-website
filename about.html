About Us

A neighbourhood bottle shop rooted in Kitsilano for nearly four decades.

Store / Heritage Photo

Since 1986

A Lot Has Changed.
Our Mission Hasn't.

Broadway Beer Wine & Spirits opened its doors in 1986, back when Kitsilano was a very different neighbourhood. Over the decades the shop has evolved — the selection has grown, the space has changed — but the reason we show up every day remains the same.


We believe that finding the right bottle should be a pleasure, not a chore. No pretension, no gatekeeping. Just a knowledgeable team who genuinely loves what's on the shelves and wants to share it with you.


Whether you're after a rare single malt for a special occasion, an everyday wine for a Tuesday dinner, or just a really good local craft tallboy — we have it, and we'll help you find it.

How We Operate

What We Believe In

🔍

Curated, Not Crowded

We taste everything we stock. The shelves are built intentionally — a mix of cult classics, small producers, and everyday essentials that earn their place.

🤝

Community First

We're a neighbourhood shop, not a chain. We know our regulars, we care about the block, and we show up for the community that has supported us for nearly 40 years.

🎓

No Snobbery

Great wine, beer, and spirits should be approachable. Our team is here to share knowledge, not flex it. Ask us anything — there are no bad questions.
